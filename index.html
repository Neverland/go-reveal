REVEAL.JS

We're big fans of reveal.js for making awesome online presentations. It was built by Hakim El Hattab.


This presentation is built in reveal.js (on slid.es).

You navigate by clicking on the arrows.

reveal.js has tons of cool functionality. You can see that functionality in action here.


We wanted to go a bit further

We thought it'd be cool to integrate reveal.js and GoInstant.

What is GoInstant?


It's a JavaScript API for building realtime, collaborative applications.


You can use it for free.


So we built
go-reveal

Go-reveal.js = reveal.js + GoInstant 
  • Drop go-reveal.js into your reveal.js presentation and instantly you have multi-user, collaborative functionality
  • Share your presentation with others (using the "share" button) and participants can interact with you!
  • The "user list" shows you who's there
  • You'll see when people click around and move the presentation
  • It's completely open source!
